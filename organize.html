Organize

Organizing, cleaning, and preparing data, both quantitative and qualitative, is a critical first step for analyzing data. Taking the time to manage your files and tidy up data could save days of work and avoid unnecessary frustration. Once the data is clean and prepared it will be easier to automate tasks, create a database, and analyze large sets of data. By data we mean both quantitative and qualitative information that consists of numeric, textual, or visual data.
